## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: How do I book a consultation?**
> You can book a consultation by calling us, emailing us, or filling out the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours.

**Q2: Do you offer online/remote services?**
> Yes! Most of our services are available both in-person and remotely via video call or email.

**Q3: What are your payment methods?**
> We accept Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer, and major Credit/Debit cards.

**Q4: Is there a free initial consultation?**
> Yes, we offer a complimentary 15-minute initial consultation to understand your needs before proposing a service plan.

**Q5: How do I get a quote for a service?**
> Contact us via email or phone with your requirements and we will send you a detailed quote within 48 hours.

**Q6: Do you sign NDAs or confidentiality agreements?**
> Absolutely. Client confidentiality is a top priority and we are happy to sign NDAs upon request.
